Main files:

1. student/login.html
   Student login page.

2. student/dashboard.html
   Student name, username, and test details page.

3. student/test.html
   84 MCQs with 30 minute timer.
   It also contains the hidden Netlify form named exam-results.

4. student/result.html
   Score and pass/fail result page.

5. assets/data.js
   Student usernames/passwords and all 84 Anatomy & Physiology questions.

6. assets/app.js
   Login, timer, test submit, result, and logout logic.

7. assets/styles.css
   Full software styling.

8. _redirects
   Netlify redirects for PHP-style links.

Upload:
Upload the full con_nursing_exam folder to Netlify, or upload the zip after extracting it.

Results:
After upload, student submissions will appear in:
Netlify Dashboard > Your Site > Forms > exam-results
